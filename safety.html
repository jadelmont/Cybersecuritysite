How to Stay Safe Online

To stay safe online, use strong passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols, recognize phishing attempts by being cautious of unsolicited emails, regularly update your software to protect against vulnerabilities, secure your Wi-Fi networks with strong passwords, and backup your data regularly to prevent data loss in case of an attack.

Jasly Delmonte, 2024
