Common Cyber Threats

Common cyber threats include phishing attacks that trick individuals into revealing sensitive information, malware designed to disrupt or damage systems, and ransomware that demands payment to restore access to encrypted files. Social engineering manipulates individuals to divulge confidential information, and DDoS attacks flood a network with traffic, making it inaccessible. Understanding these threats is crucial for implementing effective cybersecurity measures.
